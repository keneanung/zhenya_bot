PING
----

      - PING :crafting\.dangerbear\.in
   - **Sending a PONG**

MODE
----

      - :jesse\!~jesse@ada\.evergreen\.edu MODE \#greatestguys \+o willcoster

   - **Sending a MODE**
      - > MODE \#greatestguys \+o jesse
